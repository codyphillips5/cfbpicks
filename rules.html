Official CFB Picks Rules

1. FBS vs FBS teams playing on the current week's Saturday will be included in the list of eligible games.

2. You pick 5 games per week and one of those MUST be the Game of the Week (highlighted in yellow). The other four are up to you.

3. You rank your picks from 50, 40, 30, 20, 10 in confidence. These are the points you are awarded for correct picks.

4. We will have weekly winners and season long winners with payouts for both.

5. ALL PICKS LOCK @ the first game's kickoff. Typically this is noon eastern but can be earlier. It is up to each user to get their picks in ahead of the first game.

6. ALL SPREADS LOCK at noon eastern on Thursday of the current week. This typically allows for 48 hours of open time to submit picks each week.
